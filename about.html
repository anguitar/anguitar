About Aaron

Aaron Nelson is a Christian musician, worship leader, and songwriter based in Missouri. He has a passion for leading people into the presence of God through music that is both heartfelt and theologically grounded.

With a background in vocal performance and years of experience leading worship, Aaron blends powerful vocals with deep, relatable lyrics. Whether on stage or in the studio, his mission is simple: to glorify Christ and serve the Church.
